About Us

Our Story

Sai Krishna Sweets is a rich philosophy of innovative sweets. Having maintained a strong foot holds in Madhira,we have extended to Sai Krishna Sweets & Bakery. Each and every product is duly crafted by chefs having years of culinary expertise. We put whole-hearted efforts in each of our sweets & savoury to give you the taste that will linger on your mind. We guarantee that the appetising aroma of our freshly baked cakes and confectionaries will make you keep coming back for more.

Mission:

At Sai Krishna Sweets & Bakery,we’re on a mission to create happiness through our delicious Sweets & Cakes. Crafting with care and quality ingredients, we aim to make every bite a moment to cherish. From birthdays to weddings, and every special occasion in between, we’re here to sprinkle sweetness and spread smiles, one at a time.
